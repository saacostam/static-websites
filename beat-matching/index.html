The process

  1. Song Selection: Pre-selected or on the go. Based on crowd reaction and musical taste.
  2. Mixing: Make the transition* between songs sound good.

Transition*

  • Two songs have different characteristics:
    • Key
    • Velocity (Tempo, BPM)
    • Structure
    • Volume
    • Frequency Distribution
